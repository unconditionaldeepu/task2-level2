Ratan Tata Sir

28 Dec 1937

BIOGRAPHY

Ratan Tata: Visionary leader and philanthropist. Educated in architecture and business, he spearheaded Tata Motors' success with the Indica and transformed Tata Consultancy Services. Humble and innovative, he values employee welfare. Through Tata Trusts, he supports education, healthcare, and rural development. In crises like 2008 Mumbai attacks and COVID-19, he provided aid and resources, embodying his commitment to the nation's welfare. With his business acumen, compassion, and significant contributions, Ratan Tata's legacy inspires generations and uplifts India's progress..
